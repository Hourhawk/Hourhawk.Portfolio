So, what's a web developer?

Hello everyone! My name is Hourhawk, and I am web developer that streams on a platform called Twitch. However, what is a web developer and how does a site differ from a custom-built one and a site that uses templates on Wordpress and other platforms? Good question! Custom-built sites usually tend to fit the demographic in which a client is persuing to increase business and sales. Tactics can be used that improve and move your site in the right direction. Developers take into account the three key features:

  • Search Engine Optimization
  • Design that fits your business.
  • A well-rounded user experience.
